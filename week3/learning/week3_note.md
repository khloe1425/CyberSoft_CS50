**Running time** - big O notation \
![time to solve](https://cs50.harvard.edu/x/2023/notes/3/cs50Week3Slide042.png) \
*Order of running time (the worst - the fastest)*
1. O(n<sup>2</sup>) - the worst
2. O(n) - linear search 
3. O(log n) - binary search
4. O(1) - the fastest
